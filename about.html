Group session


Pilates is a great workout for women of all ages. It can help to build strength, flexibility and endurance. It can also help to improve posture and alleviate back pain. Pilates is a low-impact form of exercise, which makes it ideal for those who are new to exercise or have injuries or medical conditions that prevent them from participating in high-impact activities.

pre/post natal


Pilates is a great form of exercise for pregnant women, as it can help to improve posture and strengthen the core muscles. It is important to start slowly and gradually increase the intensity of your workouts as your pregnancy progresses. Always consult with your doctor before starting any new exercise regimen.

Hot room


Hotroom pilates is a great way to get your sweat on and improve your fitness at the same time. This type of pilates is done in a room that is heated to around 80-85 degrees, which makes for a great workout. Not only does the heat help you to sweat more, but it also helps to loosen up your muscles and make them more pliable. This makes hotroom pilates an excellent choice for those who are looking to improve their flexibility and range of motion
